Welcome to the iSchedule Home page


What is iSchedule:

If you are a student at George Mason University, then you are probably very familiar with the course scheduling process and just how tedious it can be at times. It can take a student anywhere from a couple of hours to a couple of days to schedule their classes. At times it might make you give up on college all together. Especially when you have scheduled 4 of your classes only to find out that the 5th does not fit anywhere in your schedule. Or when you quit your job because you could not find a suitable schedule.

 

We have all been through this situation, and it only becomes harder as you go through college. I thought if we could somehow magically have a list of schedules to choose from, it would make things easier. And that is what this applet is for. I wanted to make scheduling classes as easy and as fast as possible, so that you don't have to worry about it.

 

This program was designed by a George Mason student for George Mason students. So if you find any mistakes or would like to express your opinion feel free to leave me a feedback.


Why the iSchedule:

The iSchedule was created to help me and other students schedule their courses in an easy way. I ask nothing from the user except for feedback. The program is free to use and you do not have to download anything. Everything is online, and I will be adding features as I progress through college. I hope you enjoy it and that it works for you.
